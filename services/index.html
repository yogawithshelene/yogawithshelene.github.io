Services

Yoga Wellness Coaching

Private sessions that take a holistic approach to your yoga practice. It is my privilege to work together with you on an individualized basis to reach your goals. Within the framework of Adamantine® Yoga, I will teach you a customized yoga practice tailored to fit your unique needs and help you progress in a compassionate yet challenging way. We will also explore lifestyle, diet, and exercise options to optimize your practice.

Small Group or Private Yoga Sessions

Are you interested in pursuing a yoga practice but don’t know where to start? Have you wanted to attend a yoga class but would like an introduction first? I am available for small group or private sessions to help you begin your yoga journey with confidence. All yoga instruction is given in the style of Adamantine®.

For Yoga Wellness Coaching or Private Yoga Sessions, contact me to arrange a time and location that is convenient for you. Pricing is based on the hour but is also available in packages of 3, 5, or 10 sessions for a discounted rate.
